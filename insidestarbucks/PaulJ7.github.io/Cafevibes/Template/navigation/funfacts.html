Fun Facts!

Here are all the facts as stated in the video:

  • Starbucks was founded in Seattle, Washington 1971
  • There are over 87,000 drink combinations
  • When Starbucks first opened up for business they had a short size (8oz) and a tall size (12oz). Soon the short became phased out with the introduction of the 16oz. Grande and the 24oz. Venti
  • Later Starbucks would also add the 31 oz. Trenta that is only available for iced coffees, iced teas, and refresher beverages
  • Trenta, despite meaning 30, holds 31 ounces of liquid, equivalent to 916 ml. The average stomach has a volume of about 1000ml before it starts stretching. Talk about a large drink
  • Starbucks is named after the character, Starbuck, in the novel Moby Dick. He was the first mate on board the Pequod, the boat in which they sailed
  • What not many know is that Starbucks was almost named Pequod instead
